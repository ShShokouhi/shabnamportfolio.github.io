"Engineering is the closest thing to magic that exists in the world." – Elon Musk

I'm Shabnam (Sarah), a passionate Mechanical and Biomedical Engineer. I hold an M.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering and a Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering from the University of Melbourne. I thrive on quickly learning new projects and jumping in with both feet, always eager to take on new challenges. I try to bring a can-do attitude and a creative spirit to everything I do, both in my professional work and in my personal life.

To me, mechanical engineering is like a Swiss Army knife—versatile, indispensable, and ready to tackle anything that comes its way. Working as a design engineer in the oil and gas industry allowed me to roll up my sleeves and see how mechanical principles play out in the real world—a thrilling experience! I equally enjoyed my time in the biomedical engineering field, where I could apply my engineering skills to improve people’s lives (at least, I hope I did!). I genuinely love being an engineer and am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead in my career.

Outside of work, I'm an avid reader, traveler, and hiker, always seeking new ways to explore and capture the world around me :)
