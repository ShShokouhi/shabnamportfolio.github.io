Mechanical design engineer

I'm from Iran, where the oil and gas industry is huge. As a mechanical engineer, I had the opportunity to intern and work at top engineering design firms in the oil, gas, and petrochemical sectors. Right after earning my B.Sc., I interned at PIDEC, a leading consulting firm, where I gained hands-on experience in engineering design, technical drawing, and documentation. I worked closely with the mechanical team on 3D CAD models and P&ID designs, and I even got to visit an under-construction plant we were consulting on.

After completing my M.Sc. and before moving to Australia, I worked as a Mechanical Design Engineer at Siraf SRIC . I delivered 3D CAD and CAM models, engineering drawings, and documentation for numerous components and assemblies, and I also handled risk assessments on rotary equipment. It was a great experience, though I can't share more details due to confidentiality agreements.
